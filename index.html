Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about traveling with Sedra

How do I book a travel package with Sedra?
Booking with Sedra is simple! Browse our packages, contact us through our website or phone, and our travel consultants will guide you through the entire process. We'll customize the package to your preferences and handle all arrangements.
What is included in your travel packages?
Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and select meals. Specific inclusions vary by package. We provide detailed itineraries outlining everything included, and we can customize packages to add or remove services based on your preferences.
Do you offer travel insurance?
Yes, we strongly recommend travel insurance and can help arrange comprehensive coverage for your trip. This includes medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost luggage, and more. Our team will explain all available options during the booking process.
Can I customize an existing package?
Absolutely! All our packages are fully customizable. Whether you want to extend your stay, add activities, upgrade accommodations, or modify the itinerary, our consultants will work with you to create your perfect journey.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package and timing. Generally, cancellations made 60+ days before departure receive a full refund minus a small processing fee. For cancellations closer to departure, fees may apply. We recommend travel insurance to protect your investment.
Do you provide visa assistance?
Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance for all destinations. Our team will guide you through the requirements, help with documentation, and ensure you have everything needed for a smooth entry to your destination country.
